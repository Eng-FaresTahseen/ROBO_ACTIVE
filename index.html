The Challenge in Nuclear Facilities

Nuclear facilities contain complex networks of pipes, valves, and critical equipment that are continuously exposed to harsh conditions, leading to corrosion, cracks, and potential radiation leaks.

Traditional manual inspection is incredibly dangerous, costly, and inefficient. It places human lives at risk and requires extensive safety protocols.

According to the ALARA (As Low As Reasonably Achievable) principles, human exposure to radiation must be minimized at all costs. An autonomous solution is not just an upgrade; it is a necessity.

Project Objectives

Reduce Exposure

Minimize human exposure to hazardous radiation zones strictly adhering to ALARA principles.

Detect Defects

Automatically detect microscopic cracks and corrosion on critical infrastructure using advanced AI.

Environmental Monitoring

Continuously monitor radiation levels, thermal anomalies, and dangerous gas leaks.

Real-time Alerts

Generate instantaneous alerts and comprehensive reports to the centralized dashboard.

Enhance Safety

Improve overall facility safety, reduce downtime, and significantly lower maintenance costs.

Proposed Solution

CoreSentinel is an advanced autonomous robotic system built on ROS, integrating state-of-the-art AI for computer vision with a suite of precise environmental sensors.

Mechanical & Sensor Design

Robust Chassis Architecture

Designed using industrial-grade CAD modeling, the CoreSentinel features a radiation-hardened chassis, all-terrain track wheels, and stabilized sensor mounts.

  • Lead-lined protective enclosure for electronics
  • Omni-directional mobility system
  • Modular payload bays for quick sensor swapping

Instrumentation Suite

Geiger-Müller Counter

High-sensitivity radiation detection for Alpha, Beta, and Gamma rays.

Thermal Imaging

Detects overheated valves and abnormal pipe temperatures instantly.

Gas Sensors (MQ)

Monitors hazardous gas leaks in the environment (Hydrogen, Methane).

High-Res Vision

4K Camera system for optical defect detection and teleoperation.

Artificial Intelligence & Safety Compliance

Deep Learning Vision

Utilizing optimized Convolutional Neural Networks (YOLOv8 architecture), CoreSentinel processes video feeds in real-time on edge hardware.

  • Micro-crack detection on metallic surfaces.
  • Rust and corrosion instance segmentation.
  • Structural anomaly recognition.

Regulatory Compliance

Designed strictly under the guidance of Nuclear Engineering principles to ensure legal and physical compliance.

  • ALARA Principle: Completely removes human workers from high-dose zones.
  • IAEA Standards: Adheres to International Atomic Energy Agency remote inspection guidelines.
  • Fail-safe emergency retrieval protocols.
